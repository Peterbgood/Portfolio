ABOUT

Hi there! I'm Peter Good, a web developer with over 10 years of experience. I'm a proud resident of Knoxville, Tennessee, where I live with my wife and two children.

I received my formal education in Web Development at St. Pete College in Florida, which provided a solid foundation for my career. I'm a versatile web developer with expertise in a range of technologies, including HTML5, CSS, and Javascript. I'm also skilled in web design, graphic design (with proficiency in Photoshop), and the Bootstrap framework. Additionally, I have experience with accessibility evaluation (WCAG 2.1), content management systems like WordPress and Dot Net Nuke CMS, analytics tools like Google Analytics, and version control systems like GitHub.
