About Me

I’m a Front-end Web Developer with a degree from St. Pete College in Florida. My expertise spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, with advanced skills in React.js for creating dynamic, user-friendly interfaces. I’ve also worked extensively with WordPress, developing custom themes and plugins, and have experience with DotNetNuke CMS and GitHub for version control.

Beyond coding, I’m passionate about web design and accessibility, ensuring my projects meet WCAG 2.1 standards. With proficiency in Photoshop for graphic design and Google Analytics for data-driven decisions, I bring a well-rounded skill set to every project.
